気をつけて！(False Friends)

Some loanwords don't mean what you'd expect from the English.

マンション = apartment building (NOT mansion)

A large, multi-story apartment building. For "mansion" say 大きい家.

デパート = department store (NOT "depart")

Shortened from "department store." A large retail building.

バイク = motorcycle (NOT bicycle)

For bicycle, say 自転車 (じてんしゃ) — not in our V4, but good to know.

バイト = part-time job (NOT "bite")

From German "Arbeit" (work). Very common among students.

クレーム = complaint (NOT "claim")

Making a クレーム means complaining to a business.

スマート = slim/stylish (NOT "smart")

For "smart" (intelligent), use 頭が良い.
