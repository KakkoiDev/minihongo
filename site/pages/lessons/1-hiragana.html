特別なルール (Special Rules)

長い母音 (Long Vowels)

Some vowels are extended by adding another vowel character:

  • おかあさん (okaasan — mother)
  • おにいさん (oniisan — older brother)
  • くうき (kuuki — air)
  • おねえさん (oneesan — older sister)
  • おおきい (ookii — big)

小さい「っ」(Double Consonants)

A small っ doubles the consonant that follows it:

  • きって (kitte — stamp)
  • がっこう (gakkou — school)
  • にっぽん (nippon — Japan)
  • きっぷ (kippu — ticket)

特別な読み方 (Special Readings)

Two characters change pronunciation when used as particles:

  • は is read as wa when used as a topic particle: 私は学生です
  • へ is read as e when used as a direction particle: 山へ行きます
